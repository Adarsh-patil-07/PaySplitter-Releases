## ✨ Key Features

* **🌍 Global Multi-Currency Support:** Traveling the world? Instantly switch between global currencies directly from your dashboard. 
* **🧮 Smart Debt Calculation:** The app automatically does the heavy lifting, calculating exactly who owes money, to whom, and how much.
* **☁️ Real-time Cloud Sync:** Securely powered by Google Sign-In and Firebase. Your data is instantly backed up and synced across all your devices.
* **💎 Premium Glassmorphic UI:** A beautiful, state-of-the-art dark mode interface with micro-animations and sleek design elements.
* **🗑️ Trash & Recovery:** Accidentally deleted a trip? No problem! Easily recover it from the built-in Trash bin.

## 🛠️ Built With
* **Frontend:** HTML5, Vanilla JavaScript, CSS3
* **Backend:** Firebase (Authentication & Realtime Database)
* **Mobile Build:** Capacitor.js
